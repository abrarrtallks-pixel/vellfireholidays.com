Who We Are

Kashmir's Trusted Travel Partner

Vellfire Holidays is a premier tour and travel agency based in Nawakadel, Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir. Founded with a deep passion for the Valley and a commitment to exceptional guest experiences, we have been helping travelers explore the paradise on earth with ease, comfort, and confidence.

From handcrafted Kashmir tour packages — covering Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonamarg, and Dal Lake — to reliable car rentals with experienced local drivers, we take care of every detail of your journey. Our hotel booking service ensures you rest in comfort after every beautiful day in the Valley.

At Vellfire Holidays, customer satisfaction is not a goal — it is our standard. Every tour is personalised, every driver is vetted, and every itinerary is crafted with local expertise and genuine care.
